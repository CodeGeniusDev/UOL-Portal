**Fleet Size**: 108+ vehicles (buses, coasters, vans)
**Coverage Areas**:
- Sheikhupura Route — Departs: 06:45 AM
- Kasur Route — Departs: 07:00 AM
- Raiwind Route — Departs: 07:15 AM
- Lahore City (Gulberg, Garden Town, Johar Town) — Departs: 07:30 AM
- Defence / DHA Route — Departs: 07:20 AM
- Ferozepur Road Route — Departs: 07:10 AM

**Return timings**: Evening drop-offs between 3:00 PM – 5:00 PM.

## STUDENT SUPPORT & COUNSELING

- **University Counseling Center (UCC)**: Free, confidential psychological support. Offers therapy, group sessions, stress/anxiety workshops.
- **UOL Wellbeing Centre**: Mental and physical wellness programs.
- **Office of Student Affairs (OSA)**: General student help — transport, hostel, complaints, event coordination. Located EE1 Ground Floor.
- **Stress Policy**: If a student feels overwhelmed by teacher pressure or exams, they are encouraged to speak to a UCC counselor or visit OSA. Mental health is a university priority.

- **Merit-based admission** for most BS programs.
